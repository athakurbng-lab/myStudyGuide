## ðŸš€ How to Use

### Setting Up
1.  **API Key**: On the Dashboard, tap the **Key Icon** (top right) and enter your Google Gemini API Key.
2.  **Theme**: Tap the **Sun/Moon** icon to toggle between Light and Dark modes.

### Importing Content
1.  **Add Book/Quiz**: Tap the **+** button (bottom right) on the Dashboard.
2.  **Choose Source**: Select "Upload PDF" for books, or "Generate Quiz" options.
3.  **Organize**: Long-press the newly created item to **Move** it into a folder.

### Taking a Quiz
1.  Tap a quiz card to start.
2.  Select answers. You can skip questions and come back.
3.  **Submit**: Finish the quiz to get your score.
4.  **Save Progress**: If interrupted, just hit back. The status will change to "Resume", and your answers are saved.

### Reviewing Results
1.  After submitting, you'll see your Score and a "History" tab.
2.  **Double-tap** a history item to inspect that specific attempt.
3.  Tap **"Weakness Analysis"** to let the AI explain *why* you got questions wrong.

### Managing Folders
1.  **Create**: Long-press an item -> "Move" -> Tap the **(+)** icon to create a folder.
2.  **Navigate**: Tap a folder to enter. Tap "Back" or "Home" breadcrumbs to exit.
3.  **Delete**: Long-press a folder to delete it. *Warning: Items inside will be moved to Home.*
